Instructions:
Click on a piece that you want to move. Only pieces that are of the color
in turn and that can be moved are allowed to be clicked. Valid locations
to move to are marked with a red border. Game is won by taking the
opponent's king. Game can be reset with the reset button and the
window can be closed with the quit button.
